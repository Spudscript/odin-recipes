Cranberry Orange Bread

Description

A scrumptious holiday bread infused with the mild tartness of cranberry tempered by the sweet zest of orange. The perfect warm treat for a snowy winter night.

Ingredient List

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1 medium orange
  • 1 cup ground raw cranberries
  • 1 tbsp grated orange rind

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Grease and flour a bread loaf pan. Set aside.
  3. Sift flour together in a bowl with salt, baking powder, baking soda and sugar.
  4. Add grated orange rind. Set aside.
  5. Place vegetable oil in a measuring cup. Add orange juice and fill to 3/4 cup mark with water.
  6. Stir the oil/orange juice/water mix slowly in to the bowl of dry ingredients.
  7. Add lightly beaten egg.
  8. Mix in walnuts and cranberries.
  9. Pour in to the greased pan and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  10. Cool for 10 minutes before slicing.
  11. Enjoy!
