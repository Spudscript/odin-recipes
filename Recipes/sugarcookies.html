Orange Sugar Cookies

Description

Delicious, soft sugar cookies with just a hint of orange zest. Just like Gram used to make!

Ingredient List

  • 2/3 cup shortening
  • 1 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp orange juice
  • grated rind of one small orange

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream the shortening and sugar together in a bowl.
  3. Stir in 2 eggs.
  4. Slowly add in flour, salt, baking powder, orange juice and orange rind.
  5. Chill dough in fridge for one hour.
  6. Roll our dough and cut cookies.
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.
