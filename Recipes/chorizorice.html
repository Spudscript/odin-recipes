Chorizos and Rice

Description

Old world recipe from the Basque country. Savory chorizo against a backdrop of savory peppers and rice.

Ingredient List

  • 3-4 chorizos
  • 1 cup Uncle Ben's converted rice
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 7-9 oz jar of pimientos
  • 1 tbsp garlic salt (or subsitute 1 tsp salt and 3 minced garlic cloves).

Directions

  1. Cut up chorizos into 1/2 to 1 inch slices.
  2. Fill a pot with 4-6 cups of water and boil chorizos at a slow boil for 30-45 minutes.
  3. Cut up onion, green pepper and pimiento.
  4. measure 7 1/2 cups of water and include water the chorizos were cooked in.
  5. Put chorizos, rice, peppers, onion, pimiento and garlic salt in the pot of water.
  6. Bring to boil and cook on low until rice is tender, about 20 minutes.
  7. Do not overcook.
  8. Enjoy!
